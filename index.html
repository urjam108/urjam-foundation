उर्जम् (URJAM)

Renewable Energy • Sustainable Future

उर्जा जीवनस्य मूलम् अस्ति। स्वच्छ-नवीकरणीय-ऊर्जायाः प्रचारः पृथिव्याः संरक्षणाय, पर्यावरणस्य संतुलनाय च अत्यावश्यकः अस्ति। उर्जम् पहलः जनसमुदायान् स्वावलम्बिनः, पर्यावरण-अनुकूलाः तथा सतत-विकासस्य मार्गे प्रेरयति।

URJAM is the Renewable Energy Initiative of URJAM Foundation dedicated to promoting clean, sustainable and affordable energy solutions. Through awareness programs, community participation, innovation and strategic partnerships, we encourage the adoption of solar energy, energy conservation and environmentally responsible practices. Our mission is to reduce carbon emissions, strengthen energy security and contribute towards a greener and more sustainable future for all.

Vision

संस्कृतम्:
स्वच्छ-ऊर्जायाः माध्यमेन हरितं, समृद्धं, आत्मनिर्भरं च भविष्यं निर्मातुम्।

English:
To build a green, prosperous and self-reliant future through clean and renewable energy.

Mission

संस्कृतम्:
नवीकरणीय-ऊर्जायाः प्रसारं कर्तुम्, ऊर्जा-संरक्षणस्य जागरूकतां वर्धयितुम्, तथा पर्यावरण-अनुकूल-प्रौद्योगिकीं समाजे स्थापितुम्।

English:
To promote renewable energy adoption, create awareness about energy conservation, and support environmentally responsible technologies across communities.
